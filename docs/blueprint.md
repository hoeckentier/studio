# **App Name**: Local Lane AI

## Core Features:

- Smart Inventory Management: AI-driven tool to analyze sales data and predict optimal stock levels, minimizing waste and maximizing availability.
- Automated Ordering System: Automatically generate purchase orders to suppliers based on the inventory analysis.
- Customer Profile Database: Record contact information about shoppers.
- Self-Service Checkout UI: A modern, intuitive interface for customers to scan and pay for their purchases.
- Loyalty Program Integration: Incorporate customer loyalty programs, track points, and offer rewards seamlessly during checkout.
- Contact Form: Include a form for users to contact the store.
- Customer Notifications: Let store patrons know about events by email, phone and SMS.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Desaturated, earthy yellow (#B39957) to evoke a sense of natural simplicity.
- Background color: Very light, desaturated yellow (#F2F0EB) for a clean and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Muted green (#7A8A67), an analogous color to the primary yellow, providing a natural and calming balance.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif with a little warmth or personality.
- Use simple, line-based icons with rounded edges to maintain a friendly and approachable feel.
- Employ a grid-based layout for a clean and organized presentation of information.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user interaction and provide feedback, such as item added notifications and loading indicators.
